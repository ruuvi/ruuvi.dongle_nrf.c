#define APP_WDT_INTERVAL_MS   (42U*1000U)
 If watchdog is not fed at this interval or faster, reboot. When only "LE 2M PHY" and "LE Coded PHY" is enabled and they don't receive any packets, then for the first 21 seconds it will wait for packets on "LE Coded PHY", then it will switch to "LE 2M PHY" and wait for packets for the second 21 seconds. So, the minimum watchdog timeout should be 42 seconds.
